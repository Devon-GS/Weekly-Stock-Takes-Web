Upload sales and purchases CSV files to track inventory from your point-of-sale system.

Sales CSV

Expected columns: 1 (Description), 3 (Date), 6 (Quantity)

Purchases CSV

Expected columns: 0 (Description), 6 (Quantity)

How CSV Import Works

  1. Configure Settings: Go to Settings page and add product descriptions for each category
  2. Upload Files: Upload your Sales and Purchases CSV files here
  3. Match Data: The system matches CSV descriptions with your configured settings
  4. View on Stock Takes: Each category page will show calculated quantities from imported data
